What to know on this website?

    We all know that the Philippines have suffered two (2) different colonial occupation in the past century including Spanish and American Colonial periods.

    Some common Filipino Heroes we all know who fought that battles were Andres Bonifacio, Heneral Antonio Luna, Heneral Gregorio Del Pilar, etc.

    We will not discuss the Philippine heroes stated above; instead, we will show you the Unsung Filipino heroes who fought the same battles.

    They deserved to be discovered by you, readers, to be respected, and for their experiences to be acknowledged even though they are not listed in history books.
